## What can attacker sniff?

DNS queries

Unencrypted traffic (HTTP)

Packet metadata

Sometimes cookies (if badly configured)

## What attacks are possible?

Man-in-the-Middle (MITM)

Evil Twin Wi-Fi

Session hijacking

DNS spoofing

Traffic sniffing

## How do you defend?

Use HTTPS (always)

Use VPN on public Wi-Fi

Disable auto-connect

Avoid logging into sensitive accounts

Use firewall
