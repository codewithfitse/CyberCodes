1. 2️⃣ TCP vs UDP (important difference)
1.1 TCP

Reliable, Ordered, Slower

Used for:

HTTP (80), HTTPS (443), SSH (22), FTP (21)

Think: “I care if the data arrives correctly.”

1.2 UDP

Fast - No guarantee, No handshake

Used for:

DNS (53),DHCP (67/68),Games,Streaming

Think: “Speed over perfection.”
